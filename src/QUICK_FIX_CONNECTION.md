# 🚀 QUICK FIX - Supabase Connection

## 🔴 Seeing "Failed to fetch" Error?

Your Supabase project is **PAUSED**. This is normal after 7 days of inactivity.

---

## ⚡ INSTANT FIX (30 seconds)

### **Step 1: Click Banner**
When you login as admin, you'll see a yellow banner at the top:

```
⏸️ Supabase Project Paused
[Wake Up Project] [Open Dashboard]
```

### **Step 2: Click "Wake Up Project"**
- Button will say "Waking Up..."
- Waits 10 seconds
- If successful: Page refreshes automatically ✅
- If fails: Go to Step 3

### **Step 3: Manual Resume**
If auto-wake doesn't work:

1. Click "Open Dashboard" button
2. Look for yellow banner: "Project Paused"
3. Click green "Resume Project" button
4. Wait 2-3 minutes
5. Come back and refresh your Toodies app

**Done!** ✅ Supabase is now online.

---

## 💡 Important Note

### **App Works Without Supabase!**

While you fix the connection:
- ✅ All features work 100%
- ✅ Data saved to localStorage
- ✅ Nothing breaks
- ✅ No urgency to fix immediately

**Difference:**
- **Without Supabase**: Data stays in browser (one device)
- **With Supabase**: Data syncs across devices (cloud)

## 🔍 Still Not Working?

### **Run Diagnostic Tool:**

1. Login as admin
2. Click **"Diagnostic" tab** (2nd tab in dashboard)
3. Click **"Run Diagnostic"** button
4. Follow specific instructions for your issue

The diagnostic will tell you exactly what's wrong and how to fix it.
